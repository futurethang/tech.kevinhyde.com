02 — Meet Jenny

Hi, I'm Jenny.

I've been feeding people my whole life — not because I had to, but because there's nothing I love more than watching someone take a first bite and just relax. Food is how I say welcome. It's how I celebrate, how I comfort, how I connect.

I don't cook for one type of person or one type of table. I cook for your table — whoever's around it, whatever the occasion. My food is versatile, personal, and always made with the kind of care you can taste.

Whether you're booking me for a private dinner, coming to one of my classes, or swinging by Duchess for a puffed pancake and a latte — you're always going to feel at home.

🍳 Versatile across cuisines 🏠 Seattle-based, private events 🌈 A welcoming table for everyone

05 — Duchess

Find me at Duchess.

My brick-and-mortar home base — breakfast, coffee, and food that surprises you in the best way.

Duchess started as a love letter to Dutch mini pancakes — those perfect little puffed bites — and it's grown into something I'm really proud of. Come in for breakfast, stay for the grilled cheese, or try one of our creative bao bun sandwiches. The menu keeps evolving, just like me.
