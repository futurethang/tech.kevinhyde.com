# AI Cost Tracker

Visualize the environmental cost of AI requests in terms of water and electricity usage.

## Features

- Input token counts (input and output) from any AI interaction
- Select from popular AI models (GPT-4, Claude, etc.)
- See environmental impact visualized as:
  - Water usage (shown as milk jugs)
  - Electricity usage (shown as smartphone charges or lightbulb hours)
- Track cumulative usage across multiple queries
- Export usage data

## Data Sources

Environmental impact calculations are based on research from:
- Epoch AI energy consumption studies
- UC Riverside water usage research (2023)
- Published PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness) data from major cloud providers

## Usage

Enter your token counts from Claude Code, API responses, or other AI tools to see the real-world environmental impact of your AI usage.
